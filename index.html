Welcome to Share ’N’ Play

This a community to share toddler toys with families like yours because collectively we have more toys than what we have individually. Its also a great way to reduce waste and do our bit for the environment.
You need to create an account at first and then you can use those credentials to Login and start using the website.
Happy Sharing !!

How this works

This is an open to all community. You don’t have to give one of your toy’s to anyone in order to get one from them. We are sure that you will also share your toy with everyone here once your child has outgrown their toy.

View All Toys

This is where you can see everyone’s toys. If you like any specific toy, you can click on the ‘I want this toy !’ button and it will be moved over under ‘Your Toy’

Adding your toy

You can add your toy to the community by clicking on the 'Add Your Toy' button. You can add the name and the description of the toy. You can also decide whether you want to share the toy at that time or if you just want to keep it on your page. Should you decide to share the toy, it will populate under the All Toys section, otherwise it will be in Your Toys section. You can always choose the 'update toy' option and change your 'toy sharing' status.
